This was a simulated phishing attempt for educational purposes.

🔐 How to Stay Safe:

  • Always check the sender's email address carefully.
  • Hover over links to preview the real destination before clicking.
  • Never enter credentials on suspicious or unknown pages.
  • Use strong, unique passwords and enable 2FA wherever possible.

🧠 Advanced Awareness Tips:

  • Inspect Email Headers: View raw headers to trace the original source.
  • Use MXToolbox: https://mxtoolbox.com/ to analyze DNS records and headers.
  • Verify SPF, DKIM, and DMARC: Check for proper email authentication.
  • Watch for urgency: Phrases like “account locked” or “verify now” are red flags.
  • Report suspicious emails: Share with your IT/security team.

Knowledge is your strongest defense.
